About Me

Hi, my name is Nihar Donthireddy and I'm a senior in high school. I'm passionate about creating YouTube videos and helping raise funds for children with cancer.

I started my YouTube channel when I was in middle school. I wanted to create videos that would educate people, and I also wanted to use my platform to raise awareness about cancer. My channel has grown over the past few years, and I've been able to raise hundreds of dollars for cancer research and support organizations.

I'm so grateful for the opportunity to use my skills and passion to make a difference in the lives of children with cancer. I know that I can't cure cancer, but I can help to raise money for research and support organizations. I can also use my platform to raise awareness about cancer and its impact on children and families.

Thank you for your support!


Helping Kids Beat Cancer

Want to join me in making a difference? I'm raising money to benefit St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, and any donation will help make an impact. Thanks in advance for your contribution to this cause that means so much to me. More information about St. Jude Children's Research Hospital: St. Jude Children's Research Hospital is leading the way the world understands, treats and defeats childhood cancer and other life-threatening diseases. Our mission is clear: Finding cures. Saving children. Families never receive a bill from St. Jude for treatment, travel, housing or food - because all family should worry about is helping their child live. Please help St. Jude continue its mission by donating today.
